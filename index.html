Curried Shepard's Pie

A Classic Twist on a Traditional Highland Recipe

List of ingredients

  • 4 potatoes for mashed
  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 Teaspoon of cardamon
  • 1 Teaspoon of coriander
  • 1 Tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 onion
  • 1 10oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of chicken base
  • 1 cup of sweet peas
  1. Boil potatoes until mashed type
  2. Dice and cook onions until translucent. Remove onions from pan
  3. Cook ground beef until browned
  4. Add Spices, can of tomatoes, and chicken base
  5. Cook and reduce
  6. Add onions back to pot
  7. Spoon mixture onto baking dish. Use dough shell if desired
  8. Spoon mashed potatoes onto ground beef mixture
  9. Sprinkle peas onto mashed potatoes
  10. Bake on top rack on 400 degrees until potatoes are brownened
